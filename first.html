How to Use the Futuristic Text Encryptor
Welcome to the Futuristic Text Encryptor. To secure your messages, simply enter your message in the text input field, select your preferred encryption method from the dropdown menu, and click "Encrypt." To decrypt a message, paste the encrypted text, choose the same encryption method, and click "Decrypt." Enjoy the sleek, high-tech design while keeping your communications secure.
